| Option | Promise | Why it fails |
|---|---|---|

**Layout** : 5 horizontal cards stacked vertically OR 2-3 column grid (rendre selon largeur). Each card has #/title/body/ROI metric.

**Background** : `#0A0E1A`.

**Content** :

- **Label uppercase amber** : `WHAT WE BUILD`
- **H2 (ClashDisplay 56)** : Five proven automations for wealth management firms.

**Card 1 — Client onboarding automation**
- Body : Pulls KYC documents, drafts the IPS from intake notes, generates custodian paperwork, surfaces missing items for advisor review.
- ROI metric (teal) : `8 h → 2 h per new client (-75%)`

**Card 2 — RFP response system**
- Body : Reads the institutional or government RFP, retrieves matching sections from your knowledge base, drafts the response. Advisor edits the final 20%.
- ROI metric (teal) : `-60% response time, +16% win rate`²

**Card 3 — Compliance documentation review**
- Body : Parses regulatory updates, flags impacts on your existing IPSs and ADV filings, drafts amendment language. Compliance officer validates.
- ROI metric (teal) : `30–40% admin cost reduction`³

**Card 4 — Quarterly review generation**
- Body : Pulls portfolio data from custodian APIs, drafts a personalized review per client (commentary, attribution, action items). Advisor edits in 30 min vs 4 h from scratch.
- ROI metric (teal) : `4 h → 30 min per client (-87%)`

**Card 5 — Lead intelligence & advisor matching**
- Body : Researches inbound prospect (LinkedIn, public filings, industry context), produces a 1-page brief, suggests the best-fit advisor based on portfolio specialty.
- ROI metric (teal) : `Pre-call brief ready in <5 min`
